About Nuxeo World

We are pleased to invite you to Nuxeo World 2011, the second edition of our international conference on Nuxeo technology and solutions.

Whether you're a partner or a customer, Nuxeo World provides multiple opportunities to discuss ECM topics and share experiences.

Why attend?

  • Discuss ECM topics
  • Share your experiences
  • Learn how Nuxeo can impact your business
  • Take notes of best practices in the ECM field
  • Network with industry experts
  • Reach an audience tuned in to ECM and Open Source

Who should attend?

  • Developers
  • Partners
  • Customers
  • Contributors
  • Students
  • Integrators
  • Competitors (after all, we're open!)
  • Everyone interested in ECM or Open Source
